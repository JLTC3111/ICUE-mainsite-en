Nguyễn Hồng Hạnh

Dr. Hạnh Nguyễn is a distinguished expert in urban development and construction management, with a PhD in Urban and Construction Management. Her deep expertise spans the development of legal documents related to urban development, regional and urban planning, architectural and landscape design, and the strategic orientation of urban growth. With a strong focus on creating sustainable and well-governed cities, she has also led initiatives in urban development strategy and management mechanisms. Dr. Nguyễn has participated in advanced leadership programs, including Senior Civil Servant Training and international development courses organized by the World Bank in collaboration with the Incheon City Government of South Korea, further reinforcing her global perspective and strategic capabilities.
