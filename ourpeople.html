Nguyễn Hồng Hạnh Dr. Hạnh Nguyễn is an expert in urban development and construction management, holding a PhD in the field. Her work spans legal frameworks, urban planning, and architectural design, with a strong focus on sustainable and well-governed cities. She has led initiatives in strategic urban growth and management, and completed advanced leadership programs, including World Bank–supported training in partnership with Incheon City, South Korea.
